# prajñāpāramitā hṛdaya sūtra
## the heart of the perfection of wisdom

---

**oṃ namo bhagavatyai ārya-prajñāpāramitāyai**

ॐ नमो भगवत्यै आर्य-प्रज्ञापारमितायै

*oṃ! homage to the blessed and noble perfection of wisdom.*

oṃ! homage to the noble blessed perfection of wisdom.

---

**ārya-avalokiteśvaro bodhisattvo gambhīrāṃ prajñāpāramitā-caryāṃ caramāṇo**

आर्य-अवलोकितेश्वरो बोधिसत्त्वो गम्भीरां प्रज्ञापारमिता-चर्यां चरमाणो

*the noble avalokiteśvara, the bodhisattva, practicing the deep perfection-of-wisdom practice,*

the noble avalokiteśvara, the bodhisattva of compassion, while practicing the deep practice of the perfection of wisdom,

---

**vyavalokayati sma pañca-skandhāṃs tāṃś ca svabhāva-śūnyān paśyati sma**

व्यवलोकयति स्म पञ्च-स्कन्धांस् तांश् च स्वभाव-शून्यान् पश्यति स्म

*was looking at the five aggregates and saw them as empty of self-nature.*

perceived the five aggregates and saw them empty of self-nature.

---

**iha śāriputra**

इह शारिपुत्र

*here, śāriputra,*

here, śāriputra,

---

**rūpaṃ śūnyatā śūnyataiva rūpam**

रूपं शून्यता शून्यतैव रूपम्

*body is emptiness, emptiness itself is body.*

body is emptiness, emptiness itself is body.

---

**rūpān na pṛthak śūnyatā**

रूपान् न पृथक् शून्यता

*emptiness is not separate from body,*

emptiness is not other than body,

---

**śūnyatāyā na pṛthag rūpam**

शून्यताया न पृथग् रूपम्

*body is not separate from emptiness.*

body is not other than emptiness.

---

**yad rūpaṃ sā śūnyatā**

यद् रूपं सा शून्यता

*whatever is body, that is emptiness;*

whatever is body, that is emptiness;

---

**yā śūnyatā tad rūpam**

या शून्यता तद् रूपम्

*whatever is emptiness, that is body.*

whatever is emptiness, that is body.

---

**evam eva vedanā-saṃjñā-saṃskāra-vijñānam**

एवम् एव वेदना-संज्ञा-संस्कार-विज्ञानम्

*just so feeling, perception, mental formations, and consciousness.*

the same is true of feeling, perception, mental formations, and consciousness.

---

**iha śāriputra**

इह शारिपुत्र

*here, śāriputra,*

here, śāriputra,

---

**sarva-dharmāḥ śūnyatā-lakṣaṇā**

सर्व-धर्माः शून्यता-लक्षणा

*all dharmas are marked by emptiness:*

all phenomena bear the mark of emptiness:

---

**anutpannā aniruddhā amalā avimalā anūnā aparipūrṇāḥ**

अनुत्पन्ना अनिरुद्धा अमला अविमला अनूना अपरिपूर्णाः

*not arisen, not ceased; not defiled, not pure; not deficient, not complete.*

no arising, no ceasing; no defilement, no purity; no lack, no completeness.

---

**tasmāc śāriputra**

तस्माच् शारिपुत्र

*therefore, śāriputra,*

therefore, śāriputra,

---

**śūnyatāyāṃ na rūpaṃ na vedanā na saṃjñā na saṃskārāḥ na vijñānam**

शून्यतायां न रूपं न वेदना न संज्ञा न संस्काराः न विज्ञानम्

*in emptiness there is no body, no feeling, no perception, no mental formations, no consciousness.*

in emptiness there is no body, no feeling, no perception, no mental formations, no consciousness.

---

**na cakṣuḥ-śrotra-ghrāṇa-jihvā-kāya-manāṃsi**

न चक्षुः-श्रोत्र-घ्राण-जिह्वा-काय-मनांसि

*no eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, mind;*

no eye, no ear, no nose, no tongue, no body, no mind;

---

**na rūpa-śabda-gandha-rasa-spraṣṭavya-dharmāḥ**

न रूप-शब्द-गन्ध-रस-स्प्रष्टव्य-धर्माः

*no form, sound, smell, taste, touch, mental object.*

no sight, no sound, no smell, no taste, no touch, no thought.

---

**na cakṣur-dhātur yāvan na manovijñāna-dhātuḥ**

न चक्षुर्-धातुर् यावन् न मनोविज्ञान-धातुः

*no eye-element up to no mind-consciousness-element.*

no eye-element up to no mind-consciousness-element.

---

**na avidyā na avidyā-kṣayo**

न अविद्या न अविद्या-क्षयो

*no ignorance, no extinction of ignorance,*

no ignorance, no extinction of ignorance,

---

**yāvan na jarā-maraṇaṃ na jarā-maraṇa-kṣayo**

यावन् न जरा-मरणं न जरा-मरण-क्षयो

*up to no aging-and-death, no extinction of aging-and-death.*

up to no aging-and-death, no extinction of aging-and-death.

---

**na duḥkha-samudaya-nirodha-mārgā**

न दुःख-समुदय-निरोध-मार्गा

*no suffering, arising, cessation, path.*

no suffering, no origin, no cessation, no path.

---

**na jñānaṃ na prāptir**

न ज्ञानं न प्राप्तिर्

*no knowledge, no attainment,*

no wisdom, no attainment,

---

**na aprāptiḥ**

न अप्राप्तिः

*no non-attainment.*

no non-attainment.

---

**tasmāc śāriputra**

तस्माच् शारिपुत्र

*therefore, śāriputra,*

therefore, śāriputra,

---

**aprāptitvād bodhisattvasya prajñāpāramitām āśritya viharaty acittāvaraṇaḥ**

अप्राप्तित्वाद् बोधिसत्त्वस्य प्रज्ञापारमिताम् आश्रित्य विहरत्य् अचित्तावरणः

*because of non-attainment, the bodhisattva, relying on the perfection of wisdom, dwells with mind unobstructed.*

because of non-attainment, the bodhisattva, relying on the perfection of wisdom, dwells with mind unobstructed.

---

**cittāvaraṇa-nāstitvād atrasto viparyāsa-atikrānto niṣṭhā-nirvāṇa-prāptaḥ**

चित्तावरण-नास्तित्वाद् अत्रस्तो विपर्यास-अतिक्रान्तो निष्ठा-निर्वाण-प्राप्तः

*because there is no mind-obstruction, unafraid, having gone beyond error, one attains final nirvāṇa.*

because there is no obstruction of mind, there is no fear. having gone beyond delusion, they attain final nirvāṇa.

---

**tryadhva-vyavasthitāḥ sarva-buddhāḥ prajñāpāramitām āśritya anuttarāṃ samyaksambodhim abhisambuddhāḥ**

त्र्यध्व-व्यवस्थिताः सर्व-बुद्धाः प्रज्ञापारमिताम् आश्रित्य अनुत्तरां सम्यक्सम्बोधिम् अभिसम्बुद्धाः

*all buddhas abiding in the three times, relying on the perfection of wisdom, fully awaken to unsurpassed complete perfect awakening.*

all buddhas of past, present, and future, relying on the perfection of wisdom, attain unsurpassed complete perfect awakening.

---

**tasmāj jñātavyaṃ**

तस्माज् ज्ञातव्यं

*therefore it should be known:*

therefore it should be known:

---

**prajñāpāramitā mahā-mantro**

प्रज्ञापारमिता महा-मन्त्रो

*the perfection of wisdom is the great mantra,*

the perfection of wisdom is the great mantra,

---

**mahā-vidyā-mantro**

महा-विद्या-मन्त्रो

*the mantra of great knowledge,*

the mantra of great insight,

---

**'nuttara-mantro**

अनुत्तर-मन्त्रो

*the unsurpassed mantra,*

the unsurpassed mantra,

---

**'samasama-mantraḥ**

असमसम-मन्त्रः

*the unequaled mantra,*

the mantra without equal,

---

**sarva-duḥkha-praśamanaḥ**

सर्व-दुःख-प्रशमनः

*the allayer of all suffering—*

the remover of all suffering—

---

**satyam amithyatvāt**

सत्यम् अमिथ्यत्वात्

*truth, because it is not false.*

truth, not falsehood.

---

**prajñāpāramitāyām ukto mantraḥ**

प्रज्ञापारमितायाम् उक्तो मन्त्रः

*the mantra spoken in the perfection of wisdom:*

the mantra spoken in the perfection of wisdom:

---

**tadyathā gate gate pāragate pārasaṃgate bodhi svāhā**

तद्यथा गते गते पारगते पारसंगते बोधि स्वाहा

*thus: gone, gone, gone to the other shore, gone completely to the other shore—awakening, hail!*

tadyathā gate gate pāragate pārasaṃgate bodhi svāhā

---

**gate gate pāragate pārasaṃgate bodhi svāhā**

गते गते पारगते पारसंगते बोधि स्वाहा

*gone, gone, gone to the other shore, gone completely to the other shore—awakening, hail!*

gate gate pāragate pārasaṃgate bodhi svāhā

---

**gate gate pāragate pārasaṃgate bodhi svāhā**

गते गते पारगते पारसंगते बोधि स्वाहा

*gone, gone, gone to the other shore, gone completely to the other shore—awakening, hail!*

gate gate pāragate pārasaṃgate bodhi svāhā

---

**iti prajñāpāramitā-hṛdayaṃ samāptam**

इति प्रज्ञापारमिता-हृदयं समाप्तम्

*thus the heart of the perfection of wisdom is complete.*

this completes the heart of the perfection of wisdom.
