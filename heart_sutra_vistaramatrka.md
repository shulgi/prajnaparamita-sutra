# prajñāpāramitā hṛdaya sūtra (vistaramātṛkā)
## the heart of the perfection of wisdom (long version)

---

**namaḥ sarvajñāya**

नमः सर्वज्ञाय

*homage to-the-omniscient-one*

homage to the omniscient one.

---

**oṃ namo bhagavatyai ārya-prajñāpāramitāyai**

ॐ नमो भगवत्यै आर्य-प्रज्ञापारमितायै

*oṃ homage to-the-blessed-one noble-perfection-of-wisdom*

oṃ! homage to the noble blessed perfection of wisdom.

---

**evaṃ mayā śrutam**

एवं मया श्रुतम्

*thus by-me heard*

thus have i heard.

---

**ekasmin samaye bhagavān rājagṛhe viharati sma**

एकस्मिन् समये भगवान् राजगृहे विहरति स्म

*at-one time blessed-one at-rājagṛha was-dwelling*

at one time the blessed one was dwelling at rājagṛha,

---

**gṛdhrakūṭe parvate**

गृध्रकूटे पर्वते

*vulture-peak on-mountain*

on vulture peak mountain,

---

**mahatā bhikṣusaṃghena sārdhaṃ mahatā ca bodhisattvasaṃghena**

महता भिक्षुसंघेन सार्धं महता च बोधिसत्त्वसंघेन

*with-great assembly-of-monks together with-great and assembly-of-bodhisattvas*

together with a great assembly of monks and a great assembly of bodhisattvas.

---

**tena khalu samayena bhagavān gambhīrāvasaṃbodhaṃ nāma samādhiṃ samāpannaḥ**

तेन खलु समयेन भगवान् गम्भीरावसंबोधं नाम समाधिं समापन्नः

*at-that indeed time blessed-one profound-illumination named samādhi entered*

at that time, the blessed one had entered the samādhi called profound illumination.

---

**tena ca samayena āryāvalokiteśvaro bodhisattvo mahāsattvo**

तेन च समयेन आर्यावलोकितेश्वरो बोधिसत्त्वो महासत्त्वो

*at-that and time noble-avalokiteśvara bodhisattva great-being*

and at that time, the noble avalokiteśvara, the bodhisattva, the great being,

---

**gambhīrāyāṃ prajñāpāramitāyāṃ caryāṃ caramāṇa evaṃ vyalokayati sma**

गम्भीरायां प्रज्ञापारमितायां चर्यां चरमाण एवं व्यलोकयति स्म

*in-deep in-perfection-of-wisdom practice practicing thus perceived*

while practicing the deep practice of the perfection of wisdom, perceived thus:

---

**pañca skandhās tāṃś ca svabhāvaśūnyān vyavalokayati**

पञ्च स्कन्धास् तांश् च स्वभावशून्यान् व्यवलोकयति

*five aggregates them and self-nature-empty beheld*

the five aggregates—and he beheld them empty of self-nature.

---

**athāyuṣmān śāriputro buddhānubhāvenāryāvalokiteśvaraṃ bodhisattvam etad avocat**

अथायुष्मान् शारिपुत्रो बुद्धानुभावेनार्यावलोकितेश्वरं बोधिसत्त्वम् एतद् अवोचत्

*then-venerable śāriputra by-buddha's-power to-noble-avalokiteśvara bodhisattva this said*

then the venerable śāriputra, through the power of the buddha, said this to the noble avalokiteśvara, the bodhisattva:

---

**yaḥ kaścit kulaputro gambhīrāyāṃ prajñāpāramitāyāṃ caryāṃ cartukāmaḥ kathaṃ śikṣitavyaḥ**

यः कश्चित् कुलपुत्रो गम्भीरायां प्रज्ञापारमितायां चर्यां चर्तुकामः कथं शिक्षितव्यः

*who any son-of-noble-family in-deep in-perfection-of-wisdom practice wishes-to-practice how should-train*

"how should any son of noble family who wishes to practice the deep practice of the perfection of wisdom train?"

---

**evam ukta āryāvalokiteśvaro bodhisattvo mahāsattva āyuṣmantaṃ śāriputram etad avocat**

एवम् उक्त आर्यावलोकितेश्वरो बोधिसत्त्वो महासत्त्व आयुष्मन्तं शारिपुत्रम् एतद् अवोचत्

*thus addressed noble-avalokiteśvara bodhisattva great-being to-venerable śāriputra this said*

thus addressed, the noble avalokiteśvara, the bodhisattva, the great being, said this to the venerable śāriputra:

---

**yaḥ kaścic chāriputra kulaputro vā kuladuhitā vā**

यः कश्चिच् छारिपुत्र कुलपुत्रो वा कुलदुहिता वा

*who any śāriputra son-of-noble-family or daughter-of-noble-family or*

"śāriputra, any son of noble family or daughter of noble family

---

**gambhīrāyāṃ prajñāpāramitāyāṃ caryāṃ cartukāmas tenaivaṃ vyavalokayitavyam**

गम्भीरायां प्रज्ञापारमितायां चर्यां चर्तुकामस् तेनैवं व्यवलोकयितव्यम्

*in-deep in-perfection-of-wisdom practice wishes-to-practice by-them-thus should-behold*

who wishes to practice the deep practice of the perfection of wisdom should behold thus:

---

**pañca skandhās tāṃś ca svabhāvaśūnyān samanupasyati sma**

पञ्च स्कन्धास् तांश् च स्वभावशून्यान् समनुपश्यति स्म

*five aggregates them and self-nature-empty correctly-observes*

they correctly observe the five aggregates as empty of self-nature.

---

**rūpaṃ śūnyatā śūnyataiva rūpam**

रूपं शून्यता शून्यतैव रूपम्

*form emptiness emptiness-itself form*

body is emptiness, emptiness itself is body.

---

**rūpān na pṛthak śūnyatā śūnyatāyā na pṛthag rūpam**

रूपान् न पृथक् शून्यता शून्यताया न पृथग् रूपम्

*from-form not separate emptiness from-emptiness not separate form*

emptiness is not other than body, body is not other than emptiness.

---

**yad rūpaṃ sā śūnyatā yā śūnyatā tad rūpam**

यद् रूपं सा शून्यता या शून्यता तद् रूपम्

*which form that emptiness which emptiness that form*

whatever is body, that is emptiness; whatever is emptiness, that is body.

---

**evaṃ vedanā-saṃjñā-saṃskāra-vijñānāni ca śūnyatā**

एवं वेदना-संज्ञा-संस्कार-विज्ञानानि च शून्यता

*thus feeling-perception-mental.formations-consciousness and emptiness*

thus feeling, perception, mental formations, and consciousness are also emptiness.

---

**evaṃ śāriputra sarvadharmā śūnyatālakṣaṇā**

एवं शारिपुत्र सर्वधर्मा शून्यतालक्षणा

*thus śāriputra all-dharmas emptiness-marked*

thus, śāriputra, all phenomena bear the mark of emptiness:

---

**anutpannā aniruddhā amalā vimalā anūnā asaṃpūrṇāḥ**

अनुत्पन्ना अनिरुद्धा अमला विमला अनूना असंपूर्णाः

*not-arisen not-ceased not-impure not-pure not-deficient not-complete*

no arising, no ceasing; no impurity, no purity; no lack, no completeness.

---

**tasmāt tarhi śāriputra śūnyatāyāṃ na rūpaṃ na vedanā na saṃjñā na saṃskārā na vijñānam**

तस्मात् तर्हि शारिपुत्र शून्यतायां न रूपं न वेदना न संज्ञा न संस्कारा न विज्ञानम्

*therefore then śāriputra in-emptiness no form no feeling no perception no mental.formations no consciousness*

therefore, śāriputra, in emptiness there is no body, no feeling, no perception, no mental formations, no consciousness.

---

**na cakṣur na śrotraṃ na ghrāṇaṃ na jihvā na kāyo na manaḥ**

न चक्षुर् न श्रोत्रं न घ्राणं न जिह्वा न कायो न मनः

*no eye no ear no nose no tongue no body no mind*

no eye, no ear, no nose, no tongue, no body, no mind;

---

**na rūpaṃ na śabdo na gandho na raso na spraṣṭavyaṃ na dharmāḥ**

न रूपं न शब्दो न गन्धो न रसो न स्प्रष्टव्यं न धर्माः

*no form no sound no smell no taste no touchable no mental.objects*

no sight, no sound, no smell, no taste, no touch, no thought.

---

**na cakṣur-dhātur yāvan na mano-dhātur na dharma-dhātur na manovijñāna-dhātuḥ**

न चक्षुर्-धातुर् यावन् न मनो-धातुर् न धर्म-धातुर् न मनोविज्ञान-धातुः

*no eye-element up.to no mind-element no dharma-element no mind-consciousness-element*

no eye-element up to no mind-element, no dharma-element, no mind-consciousness-element.

---

**na vidyā nāvidyā na kṣayo yāvan na jarā-maraṇaṃ na jarā-maraṇa-kṣayaḥ**

न विद्या नाविद्या न क्षयो यावन् न जरा-मरणं न जरा-मरण-क्षयः

*no knowledge no ignorance no extinction up.to no aging-death no aging-death-extinction*

no knowledge, no ignorance, no extinction, up to no aging-and-death, no extinction of aging-and-death.

---

**na duḥkha-samudaya-nirodha-mārgā**

न दुःख-समुदय-निरोध-मार्गा

*no suffering-origin-cessation-path*

no suffering, no origin, no cessation, no path.

---

**na jñānaṃ na prāptir nāprāptiḥ**

न ज्ञानं न प्राप्तिर् नाप्राप्तिः

*no knowledge no attainment no non-attainment*

no wisdom, no attainment, no non-attainment.

---

**tasmāc chāriputra aprāptitvena bodhisattvānāṃ prajñāpāramitām āśritya viharaty acittāvaraṇaḥ**

तस्माच् छारिपुत्र अप्राप्तित्वेन बोधिसत्त्वानां प्रज्ञापारमिताम् आश्रित्य विहरत्य् अचित्तावरणः

*therefore śāriputra by-non-attainment of-bodhisattvas perfection-of-wisdom relying-on dwells without-mind-obstruction*

therefore, śāriputra, because of non-attainment, the bodhisattva, relying on the perfection of wisdom, dwells with mind unobstructed.

---

**cittāvaraṇa-nāstitvād atrasto viparyāsātikrānto niṣṭha-nirvāṇaḥ**

चित्तावरण-नास्तित्वाद् अत्रस्तो विपर्यासातिक्रान्तो निष्ठ-निर्वाणः

*mind-obstruction-non-existence-because unafraid delusion-gone-beyond final-nirvāṇa*

because there is no obstruction of mind, there is no fear. having gone beyond delusion, they are grounded in nirvāṇa.

---

**tryadhva-vyavasthitā sarva-buddhāḥ prajñāpāramitām āśritya anuttarāṃ samyaksaṃbodhim abhisaṃbuddhāḥ**

त्र्यध्व-व्यवस्थिता सर्व-बुद्धाः प्रज्ञापारमिताम् आश्रित्य अनुत्तरां सम्यक्संबोधिम् अभिसंबुद्धाः

*three-times-abiding all-buddhas perfection-of-wisdom relying-on unsurpassed complete-perfect-awakening fully-awakened*

all buddhas of past, present, and future, relying on the perfection of wisdom, attain unsurpassed complete perfect awakening.

---

**tasmāj jñātavyaṃ prajñāpāramitā mahā-mantro**

तस्माज् ज्ञातव्यं प्रज्ञापारमिता महा-मन्त्रो

*therefore should-be-known perfection-of-wisdom great-mantra*

therefore it should be known: the perfection of wisdom is the great mantra,

---

**mahā-vidyā-mantro 'nuttara-mantro 'samasama-mantraḥ**

महा-विद्या-मन्त्रो अनुत्तर-मन्त्रो असमसम-मन्त्रः

*great-knowledge-mantra unsurpassed-mantra unequaled-mantra*

the mantra of great insight, the unsurpassed mantra, the mantra without equal,

---

**sarva-duḥkha-praśamana-mantraḥ satyam amithyatvāt**

सर्व-दुःख-प्रशमन-मन्त्रः सत्यम् अमिथ्यत्वात्

*all-suffering-remover-mantra truth because-of-non-falseness*

the mantra that removes all suffering—truth, not falsehood.

---

**prajñāpāramitāyām ukto mantraḥ tadyathā**

प्रज्ञापारमितायाम् उक्तो मन्त्रः तद्यथा

*in-perfection-of-wisdom spoken mantra thus*

the mantra spoken in the perfection of wisdom:

---

**tadyathā gate gate pāragate pārasaṃgate bodhi svāhā**

तद्यथा गते गते पारगते पारसंगते बोधि स्वाहा

*thus gone gone to.other.shore.gone completely.to.other.shore.gone awakening hail*

tadyathā gate gate pāragate pārasaṃgate bodhi svāhā

---

**gate gate pāragate pārasaṃgate bodhi svāhā**

गते गते पारगते पारसंगते बोधि स्वाहा

*gone gone to.other.shore.gone completely.to.other.shore.gone awakening hail*

gate gate pāragate pārasaṃgate bodhi svāhā

---

**gate gate pāragate pārasaṃgate bodhi svāhā**

गते गते पारगते पारसंगते बोधि स्वाहा

*gone gone to.other.shore.gone completely.to.other.shore.gone awakening hail*

gate gate pāragate pārasaṃgate bodhi svāhā

---

**evaṃ śāriputra gambhīrāyāṃ prajñāpāramitāyāṃ caryāyāṃ śikṣitavyaṃ bodhisattvena**

एवं शारिपुत्र गम्भीरायां प्रज्ञापारमितायां चर्यायां शिक्षितव्यं बोधिसत्त्वेन

*thus śāriputra in-deep in-perfection-of-wisdom in-practice should-train by-bodhisattva*

thus, śāriputra, should the bodhisattva train in the practice of the deep perfection of wisdom."

---

**atha khalu bhagavān tasmāt samādher vyutthāya āryāvalokiteśvarasya bodhisattvasya sādhukāram adāt**

अथ खलु भगवान् तस्मात् समाधेर् व्युत्थाय आर्यावलोकितेश्वरस्य बोधिसत्त्वस्य साधुकारम् अदात्

*then indeed blessed-one from-that samādhi arising to-noble-avalokiteśvara bodhisattva praise gave*

then the blessed one arose from that samādhi and gave praise to the noble avalokiteśvara, the bodhisattva:

---

**sādhu sādhu kulaputra evam etat kulaputra**

साधु साधु कुलपुत्र एवम् एतत् कुलपुत्र

*well-done well-done son-of-noble-family thus it-is son-of-noble-family*

"well done, well done, son of noble family! it is so, son of noble family.

---

**evam etad gambhīrāyāṃ prajñāpāramitāyāṃ caryaṃ cartavyaṃ yathā tvayā nirdiṣṭam**

एवम् एतद् गम्भीरायां प्रज्ञापारमितायां चर्यं चर्तव्यं यथा त्वया निर्दिष्टम्

*thus it-is in-deep in-perfection-of-wisdom practice should-be-practiced as by-you taught*

it is so—the practice of the deep perfection of wisdom should be practiced just as you have taught.

---

**anumodyate tathāgatair arhadbhiḥ**

अनुमोद्यते तथागतैर् अर्हद्भिः

*there-is-rejoicing by-tathāgatas by-arhats*

even the tathāgatas and arhats rejoice."

---

**idam avocad bhagavān āttamanā**

इदम् अवोचद् भगवान् आत्तमना

*this spoke blessed-one joyfully*

the blessed one spoke this with joy.

---

**āyuṣmān śāriputra āryāvalokiteśvaraś ca bodhisattvaḥ**

आयुष्मान् शारिपुत्र आर्यावलोकितेश्वरश् च बोधिसत्त्वः

*venerable śāriputra noble-avalokiteśvara and bodhisattva*

the venerable śāriputra and the noble avalokiteśvara, the bodhisattva,

---

**sā ca sarvāvatī parṣat sadevamānuṣāsuragandharvaś ca lokaḥ**

सा च सर्वावती पर्षत् सदेवमानुषासुरगन्धर्वश् च लोकः

*that and entire assembly with-gods-humans-asuras-gandharvas and world*

and the entire assembly, and the world with its gods, humans, asuras, and gandharvas,

---

**bhagavato bhāṣitam abhyanandan iti**

भगवतो भाषितम् अभ्यनन्दन् इति

*of-blessed-one words rejoiced thus*

rejoiced and praised the words of the blessed one.

---

**prajñāpāramitā-hṛdaya-sūtraṃ samāptam**

प्रज्ञापारमिता-हृदय-सूत्रं समाप्तम्

*perfection-of-wisdom-heart-sūtra complete*

this completes the heart of the perfection of wisdom sūtra.
